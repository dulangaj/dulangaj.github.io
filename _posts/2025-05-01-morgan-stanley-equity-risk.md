## Overview
Designed and implemented global microservices and data pipelines used by Trading and Risk Management for risk exposure and PnL valuation. Served as SME for the prod-parallel environment, driving scalability, performance, and global coordination.

## Key Contributions

### Infrastructure & Automation
- Built automation tools that reduced manual configuration processes by 95%, cutting operational overhead and startup time by 50%.
- Modernized legacy data extraction workflows, enabling non-technical business users to self-serve and reducing turnaround time by 80%.
- Led CI/CD infrastructure migration across team systems, designing reusable templates and coordinating adoption.

### Product Development
- Implemented support for new equity product types with customization capabilities, enabling clients to trade with unique configurations.
- Added logic to differentiate between onshore and offshore Chinese currency across multiple product lines while maintaining system stability.
- Designed automated validation workflows for critical model changes, detecting issues before production rollout.

### Cross-Regional Coordination
- Coordinated with traders, developers, and legal teams across 3 regions to deliver compliant trading workflows, reducing processing steps by 50% and time by 80%.
- Planned and executed time-sensitive releases of position-tracking systems, doubling capacity and improving performance.

### Technical Leadership
- Provided L3 production support across four regions, resolving critical issues through coordination with trading desks and system teams.
- Mentored new engineers, led knowledge-sharing sessions, and created centralized documentation and onboarding guides.
- Built unit, integration, and regression tests to ensure reliability and fast releases.
- Optimized calculation performance for complex OTC products using priority-based scheduling strategies.
